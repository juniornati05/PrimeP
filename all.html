Our Heritage

The PrimeP Legacy

Founded in 2024, PrimeP emerged from a passion for the extraordinary. We believed that true luxury knows no borders — that the finest perfumes from the south of France deserve to stand alongside the most exquisite handbags crafted in the ateliers of Milan.

Our team of dedicated curators travels across continents, building relationships with master perfumers, artisan leatherworkers, and family-owned ateliers who have spent generations perfecting their craft. We bring their masterpieces to your doorstep, ensuring every product you receive carries the weight of tradition and the spark of innovation.

Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your products authentic?

Absolutely. Every product on PrimeP is sourced directly from the brand or authorized distributors. We personally verify each item's authenticity before it is shipped to you. We offer a 100% authenticity guarantee with every purchase.

How does international shipping work?

We offer free worldwide shipping on all orders over $200. International deliveries typically arrive within 5-10 business days. All duties and taxes are calculated at checkout and included in the final price — no hidden fees upon delivery.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all items in their original condition and packaging. To initiate a return, simply visit your order history and select the items you would like to return. We provide a prepaid return shipping label.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes! Every PrimeP order comes with complimentary luxury gift wrapping. You can also add a personalized message at checkout. For an additional fee, we offer premium gift boxes with satin ribbons and hand-written calligraphy cards.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your package in real-time through your PrimeP account dashboard or via our mobile app.

Do you offer loyalty programs?

Yes! PrimeP Circle members enjoy exclusive benefits including early access to new collections, members-only pricing, free priority shipping, birthday gifts, and invitations to exclusive events. Membership is free and you earn points with every purchase.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Orders can be modified or canceled within 1 hour of placement. After that, our fulfillment team begins processing your order. Please contact our concierge team immediately if you need to make changes.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we partner with Affirm, Afterpay, and Klarna to offer flexible payment options. You can split your purchase into 4 interest-free installments or choose a monthly payment plan at checkout.
